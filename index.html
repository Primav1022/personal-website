Hi, I'm Primav Li

I am a PhD candidate at the College of Design and Innovation, Tongji University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence and Data Design, and supervised by Professor Qing Chen in intelligent Big Data Visualization(idvx) Lab. Previously, I graduated from the top-notch major of Media and Communication Design at Tongji University. During my undergraduate years, I once interned at Tesla R&D, focusing on the design of the intelligent design platform. I also interned at Liquid Advertising Company, where I engaged in the research on NLP algorithms based on ChatGLM-13B. My nickname is "Primav" (inspired by the Italian word "primavera", with "era" removed, representing an endless spring).

The fields I am interested in are HCI, computer vision, and the design of creativity assistance tools based on cognitive science. I am deeply interested in human behavior and thoughts. I hope that my work can inspire and enlighten people. Moreover, I sincerely hope that my work can bring joy to others and truly serve the real world. My favorite saying is the motto of the College of Design and Innovation, Tongji University: "To Learn and create, for a meaningful life and a better world." This is also what I am striving for. I like comics and 3d modeling. I'm also a master in character modeling, and I have quite in-depth research on 3D shaders and Grasshopper. I'm fond of all kinds of media and emerging interactive methods.
