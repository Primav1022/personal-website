本草宇宙: Herbal Universe

This is my undergraduate graduation design work. It aims to materialize herbal medicine data to demonstrate the non-linear, three-dimensional, interactive and interlocking relationships among different dimensions of various medicinal herbs in herbal culture, such as the five elements, five flavors, growing seasons and meridian tropisms. In this way, it can deepen the viewers' holistic understanding of traditional Chinese herbal medicine culture and may inspire more creativity and inspiration.
